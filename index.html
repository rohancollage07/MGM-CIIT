CIIT 2023

1

International conference on Innovations in Interdisciplinary Techniques: Solutions for Real Life Applications (CIIT)

2

The conference will be hybrid mode. The authors can present their paper physically or online..

3

All accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion into UGC, Scopus, and SCI indexed subject to journals scope and quality requirements.

4

Modern technological products require multi-disciplinary skills and focused team effort of personnel from different engineering faculties. Understanding this, MGM College aims to create a “Platform for idea exchange”

5

This conference is expected to provide an opportunity to interact with a utilitarian goal. This effort is also expected to ignite ‘designed thinking’ or ‘customer centric’ approach amongst the young talent and their teacher mentors.

6

This conference shall be open to all branches of engineering as well as other allied academic streams like architecture, operation research etc.
